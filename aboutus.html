ABOUT US

2004

The beginnings in 2004 were modest, as LOTAYA was hidden at the far end of a narrow and bumpy lane in Yangon. Finding the restaurant was almost like going on a treasure hunt.

TODAY

LOTAYA is now upgraded to a more spacious place, and a new renovation also included new private rooms and halls. All rooms are decorated based on Myanmar tradional themes and better services are avaliable now.

The magic of the Place keeps going with savory fragrances from the kitchen. You will see the authentic and homely vibes from our Chef's cuisines, making us one of the most popular Burmese cuisine restaurants in Yangon.

LOTAYA will make the guests live an experience on it's own. LOTAYA offers more than what a restartant can offer. Dining at LOTAYA is a lifetime happening to remember.
